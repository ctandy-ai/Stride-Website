The problem with most ACL rehab

High-stakes rehab treated like it isn't.

Re-rupture rates exceed 25% when athletes return too early — or before they've genuinely rebuilt the capacity to perform.

"Cleared" means you hit a calendar date. It doesn't mean your knee is ready for a contested mark, a driving run, or a tackle.

The difference is objective testing. Evidence. A plan built around your sport — not a 12-week template that ends with "see how you go."

Cleared isn't the same as ready. Stride gets you to both.
